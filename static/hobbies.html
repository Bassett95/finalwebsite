The Cities

These cities all have a place in my heart, instantly when i visited them i fell in love, the food, culture and people are what makes these cities great. Brugge will always recieve a special mention from me, way back in October 2011 mighty Birmingham City ventured into europe to play a europa league game against Belgian giants Club Brugge, the people were so welcoming of the 6,000+ brummies, after a action packed game the English club won 2-1 scoring the winner with virtually the last kick of the game. A special friendship was formed that night between Birmingham City and Club Brugge.

Swansea really is the hidden gem of Britain, it's beautiful beaches, friendly people and seaside activities are what makes this small town from Wales such a fantastic place.
